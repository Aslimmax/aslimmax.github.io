Help. Healing. Hope.

It’s just a click away.

Connect-OC brings mental health resources currently available throughout Orange County for teens and young adults together in one place.

Find help

In Crisis

The OC WarmLine is a free and confidential service available 24/7 providing emotional support and resources to Orange County residents.

Call or text: (714) 991-6412

In an emergency or if you need immediate assistance call 911 or go to the nearest hospital.

Your voice. Your time. Your platform.

Talking about mental health issues isn’t easy. Too many struggle in silence. Feeling stuck. Alone.

At Connect-OC, we’re working to change that.

Connect-OC is about you. It’s about me. It’s about us. It’s about building a platform that brings teens and young adults from diverse backgrounds and circumstances across Orange County together to make our voices heard. To help others find their voice. To normalize talking about mental health and wellness. And to create a community that empowers each of us to pave a path to something better.

Join us. Connect. Share. Make a difference.
